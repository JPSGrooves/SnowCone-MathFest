What is SnowCone MathFest?

An interactive math adventure wrapped in rhythm, story, and arcade-style feedback. It runs as an app, remembers your progress in your browser, and rewards practice with XP, badges, and unlockable themes.

Modes & Zones

  • 💠 QuickServe: Solve fast, earn XP, jam with Cosmic Phil.
  • 🌲 StoryMode: Work the truck, meet math ghosts, unravel the lore.
  • ♾️ Infinity: Free play + endless combos by the Lake.
  • 😺 MathTips: Grampy P taught micro lessons.
  • ⛺ Camping: Tactile, fidget-friendly fun.

How to Use

  • Tap a zone on the map to jump into a mode.
  • Your XP and badges save automatically in your browser.
  • Practice daily, unlock themes, and keep the groove going.
