🌈 What is SnowCone MathFest?

(Use full screen mode for the full cosmic effect!)

SnowCone MathFest is an interactive math experience wrapped in rhythm, neon, and good vibes. It's a game, it's a festival, it's a playground for brains. 🎪🧠

Created for kids, nerds, chillers, and math rebels — the whole thing runs offline, remembers your progress, and lets you groove your way through problems, badges, and themes.

🗺️ Modes & Zones

💠 QuickServe: Solve fast, earn XP, stack cones.
🌲 StoryMode: Work the truck, meet math ghosts, unravel the lore.
♾️ Infinity: Free play + endless combos.
🧠 Math Tips: Ghost-taught micro lessons.
⛺ Kids Mode: Tactile, fidget-friendly fun.

📜 How to Use the App

Tap a zone on the festival map to explore different game modes.
Your XP and cones (badges) are saved automatically in your browser.
Go for mastery, unlock new themes, and enjoy the mathy ride.
