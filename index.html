What is SnowCone MathFest?

A colorful math arcade with strange little festival secrets.

Quick Start
  • Tap a zone on the festival map to play.
  • Your XP, cones, themes, and progress save automatically.
  • Open Options to edit your profile, view cones, change themes, manage save data, or play SnowCone Radio.
Play Zones
  • 🍧 QuickServe Pavilion: fast timed math runs.
  • ♾️ Infinity Lake: endless score, streak, difficulty, and vision chasing.
  • ⛺ Camping Games: tactile mini-games and chill practice.
  • 📖 Story Forest: math ghosts, choices, puzzles, and lore.
  • 😺 MathTips Village: Grampy P’s lessons, quizzes, jokes, and help.
What's New in 1.5.0

  • 🧮 QuickServe math lanes now support Add/Subtract, Multiply/Divide, Decimals/Money, Percents, Fractions, and Mixed Bag with clearer Easy, Medium, and Hard progression.
  • 🏆 QuickServe scoring is more consistent across math modes, with Mixed Bag keeping a small bonus bump.
  • ♾️ Infinity Lake received a final visual alignment pass with cleaner results, unified Setup/Again buttons, and stronger iPhone/iPad presentation.
  • 👁️ Infinity Lake Vision progress is clearer, with improved result tracking and a smoother Setup → Play Again flow.
  • 📱 iOS-native presentation polish continues across iPhone and iPad, with tighter HUD spacing, safer result screens, and more stable Capacitor app behavior.
