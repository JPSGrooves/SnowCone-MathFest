What is SnowCone MathFest?

A colorful math arcade with strange little festival secrets.

Quick Start

  • Tap a zone on the festival map to play.
  • Your XP, cones, themes, and progress save automatically.
  • Open Options to edit your profile, view cones, change themes, or play SnowCone Radio.

Play Zones

  • 🍧 QuickServe: fast timed math runs.
  • ♾️ Infinity Lake: endless score and streak chasing.
  • ⛺ Camping Games: tactile mini-games and chill practice.
  • 📖 Story Forest: math ghosts, choices, puzzles, and lore.
  • 😺 MathTips Village: Grampy P’s lessons, quizzes, jokes, and help.
